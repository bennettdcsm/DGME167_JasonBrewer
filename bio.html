Design Statement

I’m a Graphic Designer working in the Adobe suite and Aseprite, exploring where I can insert my creative vision. I try to focus on connecting designs aiming to connect with the message I need to bring. I’ve gotten used to using macropad with the programs I use to help with my general work flow. I’ve always been playing games, and while you’re reading this I’m probably either playing something new from the indie scene or the new Sonic. Metal Slug was probably my awakening into pixel art with how beautifully everything looks in that series. My awakening for graphic design came from many different areas, but the Danganronpa series I think was what made me interested in this path. My goal is to create designs that can speak to everyone.

Biography

A born Californian, Jason Brewer is a part-time student at College of San Mateo. He uses his unique vision to create pixel art and logo designs, and has a distinct passion for video games. About to graduate with an AA degree in Graphic Design Production, he plans to transfer to a 4-year program to obtain a Bachelor’s degree. Jason eventually plans to put his experience to use in the video game industry, creating designs and assets that appeal and speak to audiences. He can be reached out to at jyasonthejason@gmail.com. Give him a holler if you need a design done!
